Little red riding Hood

Little Red Riding Hood's mother gave her a basket to deliver to her sick grandma.

Her mother said to stay on the path all the way to grandma's house, not to stop, or speak to anyone.

As she was walking through the forest, A wolf spotted Little Red Riding Hood. He asked her where she was going and she told him to her grandma's up the path.

When Little Red Riding Hood got to her grandma's house, the wolf had already arrived and eaten her grandmother, however Red Ridding Hood didn't know this and entered.

When Little Red Riding Hood entered and saw her grandma she thought something was odd because her grandmother looked strange. That is when the wolf ate her aswell!

Luckily, a hunter in the forest heard the wolf and was able to save Little Red Riding Hood & her grandma by cutting open the wolf's stomach and freeing them!
